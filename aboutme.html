About Me


I'm a first-year Ph.D student in the Interactive School of Computing (in the Human-Centered Computing program) at the Georgia Institute of the Technology.
My current research is advised by Jacob Eisenstein and examines socially-aware natural language processing, with an emphasis on nonstandard orthography.
You can reach me at istewart6 at gatech dot edu. You can also find me on GitHub and Twitter.
In my free time, you'll find me making music and baking cookies.
